My Classroom Goals & Expectations

  1. Please obey all school rules!
  2. Please be respectful of your teacher and your classmates!
  3. Please be quiet in lines, hallways, and in the restrooms!
  4. Please come to class prepared with all supplies and homework complete!
  5. Please follow directions and finish all assignments on time!
